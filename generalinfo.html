General Information

Location & Directions

Zion National Park is located in southern Utah near the city of Springdale, not far from Las Vegas (163 miles), Salt Lake City (307 miles), or St. George (40 miles).

Zion Canyon Visitor Center coordinates (near Springdale): 37.200190, -112.987139

Kolob Canyons Visitor Center coordinates (near New Harmony): 37.453462, -113.225640

Fees & Passes

The following passes allow access to Zion National Park for seven consecutive days:

Private vehicle: $35

Motorcycle: $30

Person entering by foot or bicycle: $20 (under 15 is free of charge)

Depending on your plans, you may need to pay a fee for a permit.

Shuttle Schedule & Info

There are two free shuttles available to visitors to the Park:
(1) The Zion Canyon Line, or park shuttle, runs from the Zion Canyon Visitor Center to destinations including the Zion Lodge and trailheads for hikes to Angels Landing, Emerald Pools, West Rim Trail, and The Narrows. Download the Information Guide for a map of the park shuttle route.
(2) The Springdale Line, or town shuttle, provides service in the Town of Springdale. It stops at 9 locations in town and picks up and drops off visitors at the park's pedestrian/bike entrance at Zion Canyon Village. More info>
